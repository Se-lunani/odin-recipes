NyamaChoma

Ingredients

  • Live Goat
  • tomato
  • Kitungu

Steps

  1. Kill the goat
  2. Remove the fur
  3. Wash and remove the intestines
  4. Cut it into pieces that can be burnt
  5. Light fire over charcoal
  6. Place your selected part over fire
  7. Burn it ensuring not to over burn
  8. Serve
